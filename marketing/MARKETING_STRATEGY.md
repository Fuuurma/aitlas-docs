## Pricing Psychology

### Free Tier (Loss Leader)

**Purpose:** User acquisition, product hook

**Includes:**
- Unlimited Nexus chat
- Agent Store access
- Basic MCP tools

**Psychology:** "Free forever" removes friction

---

### Starter ($10)

**Purpose:** First purchase, prove value

**Includes:**
- 1,000 credits
- Priority support

**Psychology:** Low barrier to entry

---

### Pro ($50)

**Purpose:** Best value, maximize LTV

**Includes:**
- 7,500 credits (+50% bonus)
- Priority support
- Early access to features

**Psychology:** "50% bonus" feels like a steal
