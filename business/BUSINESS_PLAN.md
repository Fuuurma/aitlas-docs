## Executive Summary

Furma.tech is a **digital holding company and venture studio** operating across two verticals:

1. **Standard B2B SaaS** - Cash flow engines for traditional industries
2. **Aitlas Ecosystem** - AI-native agentic products (future growth)

**Model:** 100% bootstrapped, no VC funding  
**War Chest:** €5,000  
**Runway:** 12+ months

---

## Corporate Structure

### Legal Entity: Estonian OÜ (via e-Residency)

**Why Estonia:**
- 0% corporate tax on retained profits
- Revenue reinvested tax-free (servers, R&D, infrastructure)
- Personal tax only on dividends (paid in Spain)
- European VIES VAT number for B2B invoicing

**Cost:** ~$1,000/year (Xolo/Companio)

### Vertical B: Aitlas Ecosystem (Growth)

**Monetization:** Zero-burn BYOK model

| Revenue Stream | Model | Details |
|----------------|-------|---------|
| **Compute Credits** | Usage-based | f.xyz actions burn credits |
| **Credit Packages** | One-time | $10-50 per purchase |
| **f.decloy** | Subscription | $25-100/mo (MicroVM rental) |
| **Agents Store** | Rev-share | 30% cut on premium agents |

**Pricing:**
- $10 = 1,000 credits
- $25 = 3,000 credits (+20% bonus)
- $50 = 7,500 credits (+50% bonus)

**Projected (Month 12):** $10,000 MRR combined

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Cash Flow Foundation (Months 1-3)

**Goal:** €3,000/mo B2B MRR

**Tactics:**
- Hire commission-only sales rep (Catalonia)
- Walk-in demos (Sitges, Barcelona)
- TheFork/Viator integrations as differentiator

**Success Metric:** 40 paying businesses

---

### Phase 2: Agentic Wedge (Months 4-6)

**Goal:** 500+ free users, $500 credit sales

**Tactics:**
- Launch f.library + f.rsrx as standalone tools
- Open-source one MCP server (top-of-funnel)
- Build developer community

**Success Metric:** 500 active users

---

### Phase 3: Hub Activation (Months 7-9)

**Goal:** Launch Nexus, 5,000 waitlist

**Tactics:**
- Product Hunt launch (#1 Product of the Day target)
- "God-Mode Demo" viral video (2 minutes)
- Open beta (first 500 users)

**Success Metric:** 500 beta users, $2,000 credit sales

---

### Phase 4: The Economy (Months 10-12)

**Goal:** $10,000 combined MRR

**Tactics:**
- Open Agents Store (third-party developers)
- Revenue share model (70/30 split)
- Enterprise credit packages

**Success Metric:** $10,000 MRR, 70% retention
