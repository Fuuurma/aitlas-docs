## Part 1: Business Plan

### Executive Summary

Furma.tech is a **digital holding company and venture studio** operating across two verticals:

1. **Standard B2B SaaS** - Cash flow engines for traditional industries
2. **Aitlas Ecosystem** - AI-native agentic products (future growth)

**Model:** 100% bootstrapped, no VC funding  
**War Chest:** €5,000  
**Runway:** 12+ months

---

### Corporate Structure

#### Legal Entity: Estonian OÜ (via e-Residency)

**Why Estonia:**
- 0% corporate tax on retained profits
- Revenue reinvested tax-free (servers, R&D, infrastructure)
- Personal tax only on dividends (paid in Spain)
- European VIES VAT number for B2B invoicing

**Cost:** ~$1,000/year (Xolo/Companio)

---

### Capital Allocation (€5,000)

|----------|--------|---------|

### Revenue Model

#### Vertical A: B2B SaaS (Cash Engine)

**Products:**
- restauManager (€99/mo)
- tours/Guides (€79/mo)

**Target:** 40 local businesses (Catalonia)  
**Projected MRR:** €3,000/mo  
**Sales Rep Commission:** 20-30% recurring

**Purpose:** Covers all operational costs indefinitely

---

#### Vertical B: Aitlas Ecosystem (Growth)

**Monetization:** Zero-burn BYOK model

| Revenue Stream | Model | Details |
|----------------|-------|---------|
| **Compute Credits** | Usage-based | f.xyz actions burn credits |
| **Credit Packages** | One-time | $10-50 per purchase |
| **f.decloy** | Subscription | $25-100/mo (MicroVM rental) |
| **Agents Store** | Rev-share | 30% cut on premium agents |

**Pricing:**
- $10 = 1,000 credits
- $25 = 3,000 credits (+20% bonus)
- $50 = 7,500 credits (+50% bonus)

**Projected (Month 12):** $10,000 MRR combined
